Fig. 3 Effects of diploid and tetraploid Jiaogulan seed extracts (10 μg/mL) on the expression of IL-1β (A), COX-2 (B) and TNF-α (C) mRNAs in the LPS-stimulated mouse RAW 264.7 macrophage cells. DJS and TJS represent diploid and tetraploid Jiaogulan seed extracts, respectively. The vertical bars represent the standard deviations (n = 3), and different letters represent significant differences (P < 0.05).
